# Chapter 1: Introduction to Large Language Models (LLMs)

## Overview

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts behind Large Language Models (LLMs). It explains how LLMs work, their evolution, core components, training process, and real-world applications. The chapter is designed for beginners and provides the foundation required for understanding modern transformer-based language models.

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

- Understand what Large Language Models are.
- Learn the evolution from traditional NLP to transformer models.
- Differentiate between AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, and LLMs.
- Understand the transformer architecture at a high level.
- Learn the stages of LLM development:
  - Pre-training
  - Fine-tuning
  - Inference
- Identify common applications of LLMs.

## Topics Covered

- Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning and Deep Learning
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- What are Large Language Models?
- Evolution of Language Models
- Transformer Architecture Overview
- Tokenization Basics

- Attention Mechanism (Introduction)
- Pre-training vs Fine-tuning
- Inference Process
- Popular Open-Source and Commercial LLMs
- Applications of LLMs
- Challenges and Limitations
- Future Directions
